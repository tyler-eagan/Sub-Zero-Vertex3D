Today’s reality

3D is locked in engineering. Everyone else works around it.

For most appliance manufacturers, every design or service conversation still depends on exporting, converting, and emailing CAD files. That slows decisions and makes it hard to protect IP across premium dealer and installer networks.

Engineering & product teams

Design reviews revolve around screenshots and heavy CAD sessions.

  • Large fridge and range assemblies are too heavy for most laptops outside engineering.
  • Version confusion when files move through email and shared drives.
  • Upstream partners need input, but you don’t want to ship them full CAD.

Factory build & custom paneling

Complex, configurable SKUs demand accurate, visual build context.

  • Operators rely on static PDFs that lag what’s actually in PLM.
  • Panel-ready and built-in variants make instructions harder to follow.
  • New hires learn from tribal knowledge instead of intuitive 3D views.

Service & diagnostics

Techs and call centers often don’t see the true installed configuration.

  • Identifying the right part can take multiple calls or visits.
  • Limited visuals for guided troubleshooting or “show me” support.
  • Repeat visits when the wrong part shows up at the customer’s home.

Security & brand risk

Every exported CAD assembly is another uncontrolled copy to track.

  • Files on dealer and contractor laptops are hard to monitor or revoke.
  • Difficult to enforce zero-trust and export-control policies.
  • Premium design IP is more exposed than it needs to be.
The Vertex approach

Stream context-aware pixels from PLM to any Sub-Zero stakeholder.

Vertex keeps CAD geometry and metadata in a secure cloud, then streams an interactive 3D experience to any browser. Engineering, manufacturing, service, and partners all see the same truth—without ever receiving a copy of the model.

No local files

IP stays with Sub-Zero, even as access expands.

CAD, BOM, and PMI are rendered in the cloud. Dealers, installers, and internal teams work with pixels only—no STEP files wandering around the ecosystem.

  • Supports zero-trust and export-control practices.

  • Audit trails capture who saw what and when.
