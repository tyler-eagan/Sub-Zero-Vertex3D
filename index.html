Today at most OEMs

3D is locked inside engineering. Everyone else works around it.

When every collaboration step depends on exporting, converting, zipping and emailing CAD, decisions slow down and IP risk goes up. The pattern is the same across engineering, factory, and supplier programs.

Engineering reviews

Design reviews depend on sending heavy CAD files or screenshots around.

  • Large assemblies too big for most laptops.
  • Version confusion across email threads and shared drives.
  • Expensive workstations and licenses for basic reviewers.

Factory & service operations

Operators and technicians rarely see accurate 3D for the configuration in front of them.

  • Static PDFs that lag PLM by weeks or months.
  • Time lost hunting for the right view or part number.
  • Training new hires without intuitive, interactive visuals.

Supplier collaboration & RFQ

Sharing 3D with suppliers is slow and risky.

  • Manual packaging and translation into neutral formats.
  • Multiple tools and portals just to review one design.
  • Uncontrolled file copies sitting outside your network.

Security & governance

Every file you export is another copy to track, revoke, and audit.

  • IP scattered across laptops, emails, and third-party tools.
  • Difficult to enforce zero-trust and export control policies.
  • Compliance teams fighting a losing battle against “one more copy”.
